Our Heritage

“Sang Collection celebrates the beauty of Indian craftsmanship blended with modern fashion aesthetics. Inspired by Kashmiri artistry, desi romance, and contemporary ethnic culture, we create statement pieces designed for modern women who love tradition with confidence.”

The Craft

The Art of Kashmiri Elegance

Every thread tells a story of survival and beauty. Our artisans in the heart of Srinagar preserve centuries-old techniques, hand-weaving pashmina and silk with a precision that defies the fast-fashion era. It is a slow, meditative process where a single shawl can take months to perfect.

Evolution

Designed for Modern Souls

Tradition isn't a museum piece; it's a living, breathing pulse. We redefine ethnic wear by stripping away the unnecessary and focusing on silhouette, drape, and the raw emotion of the fabric. Sang pieces are made for the boardroom, the gala, and the quiet moments of self-expression.
